Victoria’s Secret

Semi-annual sale journeys spanning email, social, app and landing pages. This campaign focused on mobile-first creative, thumb-stopping imagery, and clear promotional framing to lift click-through and conversion.

← Back to Brands
Mobile Feed Creative Primary Device Facebook / Instagram Feed

The hero creative leans into rich color, bold typography, and a clear promotional hook (“$10 OFF full-priced bras”) while still feeling aspirational. Placement in the feed was tuned for thumb-stopping impact and quick comprehension of offer + CTA.

Promo Email Layout Supporting Device Tablet / Desktop View

Email layouts carried the same hero art, but added copy depth for style details, size range, and redemption info. Modules below the fold cross-promoted matching sets and coordinating categories to increase average order value.

App / Landing Page Hero Supporting Device MacBook / Laptop View

The app and landing page hero reiterated the core message, but simplified surrounding UI to keep focus on product and CTA. All three devices — feed, email, and landing — worked together to reinforce the same creative story no matter where the customer entered the journey.
