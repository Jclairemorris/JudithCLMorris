App / landing page hero

Semi-annual sale journeys spanned email, social, app, and landing pages. The hero creative leans into rich color, luxe styling, and a clear promotional hook (“$10 OFF full-priced bras”) while still feeling aspirational.

Behind the scenes, journeys were set up to target recent bra purchasers, lapsed shoppers, and high-intent browsers. Messaging across feed, email, and app reinforced the same core offer while allowing device-specific framing for click-through and conversion.
