Victoria’s Secret

← Back to Brands

Semi-annual sale journeys spanning email, social, app and landing pages. This campaign focused on mobile-first creative, thumb-stopping imagery, and clear promotional framing to lift click-through and conversion.

Mobile Feed Creative · Primary Device

The hero creative leans into rich color, bold typography, and a clear promotional hook (“$10 OFF full-priced bras”) while still feeling aspirational. The CTA and price lock in the offer; the model choice and styling keep the tone luxe instead of discount-bin.

Behind the scenes, this journey connected to triggered and scheduled email, on-site promo modules, and in-app messaging to reinforce the offer across channels. Audience segments were built around recency/frequency of bra purchases and intent signals from site browse behavior.
